Welcome to your EmmaAI projectEmmaAI is an agentic intelligence platform designed to synchronize enterprise ground truth with the global AI economy. It utilizes autonomous loops to detect brand hallucinations and execute real-time remediation across search engines and AI models.

Project infoURL: https://lovable.dev/projects/emma-ai

Core Intelligence Modules

Joir Codex (Agentic Generative Layer)
Joir is the primary execution agent for Large Language Model (LLM) synchronization and text generation.
AEO Synthesis: Automated generation of product descriptions, metadata, and JSON-LD schema optimized for AI indexing (AEO).
Autonomous Remediation: Real-time cross-referencing between detected hallucinations and the "Golden Record" to draft corrective txt signals.
Intent-Aware Content: Dynamic generation of product narratives based on Synthetic Personas (e.g., Budget, Pro, DIY).

Impact Engine (Non-Profit & Social Integration)
EmmaAI bridges enterprise-grade brand protection with social impact through a dedicated engagement framework.
The Engagement Loop: A proactive "Ask-Thank-Report" cycle for donor and customer relationship management.
Mission Integrity: Protects non-profit ad spend from biased AI recommendations, ensuring mission-critical messaging remains accurate.
Unified Source Aggregation: Ingestion of Social, Web, Ad, and Internal Database signals to maintain a single source of truth for mission impact.
